Mixing Colors

Knowing how hex works makes it easier to find the perfect color for your website or app.

If you want yellow, but ffff00 is too yellow, you can give it 1 level more red with ffff01. You can also make it 1 level less green with fffe00, or 16 levels less green with ffef00.
