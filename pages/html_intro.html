Introducing HTML

Every webpage is written in HTML. The M stands for markup.

A school teacher marks up your homework. They use a red pen to mark notes on you wrote, like This part is really good, or This part should be emphasized.

Writing HTML is also marking notes on what you wrote. The notes tell computers the purpose of each part of what you wrote.
